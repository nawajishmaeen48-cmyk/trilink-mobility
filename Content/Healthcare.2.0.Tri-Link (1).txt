Our Services – Recruitment & Relocation Made Simple

As a full-service recruitment and relocation partner,
we support clinics, hospitals, and nursing homes in Germany throughout the entire hiring and integration process for:

	•	Registered Nurses
	•	Medical Professionals (Doctors, Anesthetists, Specialists, etc.)
	•	Caregivers / Nursing Assistants

Our services include:

	•	Pre-selection and presentation of qualified candidates
	•	Organization and coordination of job interviews
	•	Explanation and clarification of employment contracts until signature
	•	Fast-track D-Visa procedures with pre-approval from the German Employment Agency
	•	Recognition of professional qualifications via ZAV or competent federal state authorities
	•	International relocation and transfer support
	•	Assistance with residence permits after employment start
	•	Support with housing search (if required)
	•	Guidance on insurance matters
	•	Dedicated point of contact until the multi-year residence permit is granted

Our goal is to make international recruitment reliable, compliant, and predictable
for clinics, hospitals, nursing homes, and healthcare professionals alike.
